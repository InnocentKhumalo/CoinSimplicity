    User Guide

      Scan Coins

    • Tap the "Capture Coin" button to open to open the phone camera
    • Place the coins on a flat surface
    • Align the coin with the camera viewfinder
    • Tap the screen to capture the coins image
    • Wait for the app to recognize and process the coins

      View Coin History

    • Tap the "History" icon at the bottom
    • View a list of scanned coins
    • Tap a coin to view its details

      Use The Calculator

    • Tap the "Calculator" icon at the bottom
    • Enter the coin's value and quantity
    • Tap "=" to calculate the total value

      Access Settings

    • Tap the three dots on the top right corner
    • Select "Settings" icon at the bottom
    • Adjust app sttings e.g.,Camera settings, notification preferences

      Navigate The App

    • Tap the bottom tabs to switch between History, Calculator, and Settings
    • Tap the three dots for additional options
    • Tap thee "Capture Coin" button to scan a new coin
